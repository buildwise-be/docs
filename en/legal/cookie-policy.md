## II. What is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on the hard drive of your computer or mobile device which is sent by the server of the website you are visiting.

The cookie contains a unique code which enables its sender to recognise the terminal concerned (your computer or mobile device) each time the website is visited.

The cookie identifies your terminal's browser, but never you personally. Furthermore, the server of a website can only read the cookies it has itself placed and has no access to any other information on your computer or mobile device.

Cookies can either be placed by the server of the website you are visiting, i.e. "first-party cookies" or by partners with whom that website collaborates, i.e. "third-party cookies".

## III. Validity Period of a Cookie

Some cookies expire when you close your browser "session cookies", whilst others remain longer on your terminal, sometimes even until you delete them manually "persistent cookies".

Generally speaking, cookies have a limited validity period. The most common validity period is 30 days and the maximum period is 13 months from the day the cookie is placed on your terminal.

## IV. General Purpose of Cookies

The primary purpose is to ensure more efficient and faster browsing (e.g. a cookie remembers the language you choose, identifies you when you access your account, etc.). Cookies store your preferences and thus enable you to speed up and facilitate your access to the site.

They also allow us to understand how you browse our website.

In addition, they offer the possibility of making the content of a website or the advertising present on that site more relevant to you. The site will therefore be adapted to your personal tastes and needs.

## V. Cookie Management

In order to place certain cookies on your terminal, it is necessary that we obtain your prior and explicit consent. We obtain this by means of the banner placed on the home page of the website.

Moreover, most browsers use cookies. You can also delete these cookies or refuse their installation at any time and free of charge by changing the options of your browser software. The configuration of each browser is different. Each procedure is described in the help menu of your browser. To do this, please go to the following links:

## VI. Types of Cookies

### a. Technical Cookies

These cookies, which contain only your IP address, are essential for visiting the site.

They enable you to:

* browse the different pages of the website and benefit from its functionalities;
* complete forms;
* ensure verification of your identity when you access your personal data;
* secure access to the website

These cookies do not require your consent since they enable you, in particular, to access the website.

### b. Functional Cookies

These cookies facilitate and analyse the operation of our website. They enable us to make your browsing experience more pleasant and more personalised.

They enable us to:

* personalise services (language, currency, location, browsing data, etc.);
* remember your choices from a previous visit;
* collect information from online forms;
* compile statistics;
* Analyse the use of the site.

Like technical cookies, these cookies are used to improve your browsing and do not require your consent.

### c. Advertising Cookies

These cookies give us information regarding your browsing habits and allow us to adapt the advertising content of our website to your interests.

Based on these, we can analyse the effectiveness of advertising campaigns, in particular by identifying the subsequent actions you take by clicking on these advertising banners. Furthermore, on the basis of geolocation data, we can adapt the advertisements that are made to you to inform you of the opportunities available near where you are.

These advertising cookies can also enable us to send you targeted advertising messages using technologies that combine data according to the sites you have visited.

The information that we collect and share only enables us to identify your terminal.

For these cookies, your consent is necessary and we request it when you access our website.

### d. Analytical or Performance Cookies

These cookies are used to collect information on the way in which you visit our website. They enable us to measure the audience for the different content and sections of our website in order to improve them.

These cookies enable us to detect browsing problems and improve the user-friendliness of our website.

These data are aggregated and are therefore completely anonymised.

For these cookies, we need your consent.

### e. Social Network Cookies

"Social network" cookies share the content that you choose to share via application buttons which belong to social networks. When you use these buttons, a third-party cookie is installed on your terminal and if you are connected to the social network, this information will be added to your profile.

Regarding these cookies, it is advisable to check on each social network what the extent of the data collection is and the use thereof.

These cookies also require your consent.

### f. Third-Party Cookies

These cookies are placed by third parties on our website and then on your terminal. We do not have control over them. To find out more, please consult the information provided by these third-party sites on their own cookies.
