  <Accordion title="I have never used AI, how do I get started with Tooli?">
    Welcome! You're about to discover how AI can make your work faster and easier.

    Watch our 2-minute getting started video - it shows you everything you need to know: How to make your account, how to ask your first question with a single click, upload documents, and use Tooli's specialized agents. You'll be up and running in no time! Ready to get started?

    [Watch the video](/en/welcome/what-is-tooli)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is this the same as ChatGPT?">
    No. While Tooli uses similar underlying technology, it is specifically built for the Belgian construction sector.

    The main differences are:
    * **Industry knowledge** | Tooli is shaped by organizations like Embuild, Bouwunie, Constructiv and Buildwise, meaning it can use specific sources for members in a way that ChatGPT cannot.
    * **Data privacy** | Unlike free versions of common AI tools, Tooli is hosted in Europe and does not use your data to train its models.
    * **Specialized agents** | It includes tools specifically designed by organization experts to use AI in the most efficient way for your construction needs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will Tooli replace experts?">
    No. Tooli is designed to remove friction, not people. It handles the time-consuming admin work, like cross-checking documents, summarizing notes, and searching for regulations so that you can focus on the technical execution. Think of it as a highly efficient AI platform; the final professional judgment and responsibility always remain with you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need training to use it?">
    No. Tooli is built to be intuitive. If you can send a text message or an email, you can use Tooli.

    You don't need to learn complex terms. Just ask your questions in plain language (even with your voice by using the microphone button), upload your files, and Tooli will guide you through the process.

    Interested in learning more? Several partner organisations offer courses on AI, where Tooli can be used and discussed. For more information on this, please visit: [www.tooli.be](https://www.tooli.be/)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data safe?">
    Yes. Security is a top priority for Tooli and its partner organizations. All data is stored on secure servers within Europe. We follow strict European data protection regulations, such as the GDPR. Your files and conversations are never used to train the general AI models. Your business data stays yours.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
