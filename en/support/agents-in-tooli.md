  <Accordion title="What are agents?">
    Agents are specialized AI assistants, each instructed to perform specific construction tasks very well. Think of them as digital colleagues with permanent roles:

    - **General Agent** -- Your go-to assistant for construction questions, standards, and document interpretation
    - **Offer Coach** -- Reviews your draft quotes and spots unclear items, missing information, and improvement opportunities
    - **Tender Checker** -- Scans tender documents and BoQs for risks, duplicates, inconsistencies, and unclear units
    - **Report Writer** -- Turns rough notes or transcripts into clean, structured reports with decisions and action items

    Unlike generic AI chat, agents maintain their expertise and use consistent, professional methods. You can switch between agents at any time, and Tooli keeps your context across the conversation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why does the General Agent redirect me?">
    This is normal, Tooli recommends the specialist or organization that fits your task or question best.
  </Accordion>

    You ask your question in natural language, after which Nexo searches validated Buildwise sources, such as:

    - Technical Information Notes
    - Dimensioning methods
    - Standards and regulations
    - FAQs
    - Innovation Papers, articles and research documents
    - All pages of the Buildwise website

    Nexo then provides a clear answer with source references.
