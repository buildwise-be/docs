## Tooli

Tooli is an AI platform that helps with day-to-day work in Belgian construction.

You can use Tooli to ask questions, understand documents, and clarify technical information related to your projects. It is designed to save time and reduce administrative work.

<Card title="AI" icon="brain-circuit">
AI stands for "artificial intelligence".
It is a new type of computer software that can understand and respond to questions and information in a way that we think is intelligent (but by a computer program: Artificially), and is the basis of tools like ChatGPT.

The most common use of AI, in Tooli and ChatGPT, works through conversation. You ask a question, attach a photo or document, get an answer, and can ask follow-up questions. This makes it very useful for tasks like (re)reading, rewriting, translating, and analysing documents or images. For example, you can give a large document, and ask for a summary, or you can ask for an explanation of a technique or material.

AI is very flexible, but also has limits. It supports your work, but it does not replace professional judgement or an expert.

You can learn more about this in [Tooli, AI and Data](/en/support/tooli-ai-and-data).
</Card>

## What makes Tooli special?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Security and data handling" icon="shield-check">
    Tooli is managed in the EU, is well-protected, GDPR-compliant, and does not use your data for training.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Specialised construction agents" icon="users">
    Tooli offers specific assistants for specific tasks such as general assistance, offer review, tender analysis, and reporting.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Long document understanding" icon="file-upload">
    Tooli is set-up to handle multi-page PDFs, Word files, Excel sheets, images, and scanned document very well.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Intuitive and safe" icon="wrench">
    While offering the main functionalities, Tooli tries to cut down the overly complex options and pre-selects the settings that work best in our construction context.
